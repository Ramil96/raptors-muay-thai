The Sport

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, known as the "Art of Eight Limbs," is a traditional martial art from Thailand utilizing punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes. Originating as "Muay Boran" centuries ago, it served as a combat technique for Siamese soldiers. Evolving into a regulated sport in the early 20th century, it features the ceremonial "Wai Kru Ram Muay" dance to honor teachers and ancestors. Training includes shadow boxing, pad work, bag work, sparring, and conditioning, making it a rigorous full-body workout. Globally popular for its effectiveness in fitness and MMA, Muay Thai offers both physical and mental benefits, attracting practitioners of all levels. Join us to experience this ancient and respected martial art.
