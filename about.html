WHO WE ARE

ABOUT US

Raptors Muay Thai, established for over 18 years in Sutton, Surrey, offers both competitive and non-competitive students high-standard training in a purpose-built facility. Our gym features two large matted areas, 21 punch bags, Thai Boxing pads, and diverse fitness equipment. We provide a variety of fitness and technical classes for all levels, ensuring something for everyone. Whether you aim to compete, learn Muay Thai, or simply enjoy a new hobby, our classes cater to your needs. For fitness enthusiasts, we offer unique classes like Box Hiit and Conditioning Circuits, promising a distinctive fitness experience in Sutton and Surrey.

Box-Hiit

This is our most popular class, featuring bag work combos and high-intensity exercises like burpees and mountain climbers, all at a fast pace to get you fit and burn calories. Suitable for all fitness levels and both genders, no boxing experience is required. Our participants range from 16 to 60 years old, so it's never too late to start!

Conditioning

This class is designed to open up them lungs and get that heart pumping and work that cardio system to the max! Different plans are made each week to really change this class up but nearly all of it is done with a partner as we find it makes everyone work that bit harder!

Muay Thai

At our Muay Thai classes, you will refine and perfect your Thai techniques and skills. Our experienced instructors will teach you how to execute Thai-style kicks, punches, elbows, and knee strikes with precision. You will also learn the art of timing and strategy, understanding when to deploy each technique effectively during a fight. These classes are designed to build your confidence, enhance your physical fitness, and deepen your appreciation for the traditional art of Muay Thai. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced practitioner, our classes will challenge and inspire you to reach your full potential.

1 to 1 and Group PT

Personal Training Whether its fitness training or Muay Thai we have several personal trainers at Raptors who specialise in a variety of these fields. We have a personal training studio complete with free weights, benches, squat rack, slam balls, resistance bands, sledge, battle ropes, assault bike, rower and more! We have both male and female fitness trainers so if the more personal touch is what you are looking feel free to contact us!
